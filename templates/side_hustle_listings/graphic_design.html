How to Get Started as a Graphic Designer:

Begin by building a portfolio showcasing your best work, including both personal projects and any professional work you've done. Familiarize yourself with design software and stay updated with current trends. Consider freelance projects to gain experience, and network with potential clients on social media and design platforms. Continuous improvement and skill-building will help establish a successful graphic design career.
