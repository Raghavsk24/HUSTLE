Survey Taker

As a Survey Taker, you'll complete online surveys that gather consumer opinions for market research. This flexible side hustle can be done anytime and anywhere.

Skills a Survey Taker Needs:

  • Attention to detail for accurate responses.
  • Honesty and reliability to maintain credibility.
  • Basic internet skills to navigate survey platforms.
  • Time management to complete multiple surveys efficiently.
  • Patience for some longer surveys.
  • Comfort with repetitive tasks to stay motivated.
  • Basic demographic knowledge to match survey criteria.
How to Get Started as a Survey Taker:

Register on survey sites like Swagbucks, Survey Junkie, or Pinecone Research, where you can earn cash or gift cards for completing surveys. Make sure to complete your profile fully so you’re matched with relevant surveys. Set aside small blocks of time to complete surveys daily, as they can fill up quickly. Keep an eye out for higher-paying surveys based on your demographics. This is a low-effort side hustle that’s easy to fit in around classes or studying.
